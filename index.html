Bridging The Gap for Bharat Founders

What is Karo Pitch?

Thousands of founders are building amazing, real businesses across the country but lack access to investors and visibility. Karo Pitch connects these hidden gems with top investors through curated experiential pitch events and a robust discovery platform.

Our Mission

Our long-term vision is to build India’s most accessible startup discovery and funding platform for Bharat entrepreneurs, focusing aggressively on inclusion and opportunity outside the traditional major tech hubs.

Why We Created It

Over the past 5 years at KaroStartup, we've shared thousands of founder journeys. We realized that storytelling isn't enough; founders need actionable capital and structured mentorship. Karo Pitch is the bridge turning stories into actionable investments.

How It Works

A simple, transparent 4-step process to get your startup funded.

01

Apply with Pitch Deck

Submit your application and initial pitch deck outlining your business model, traction, and vision.

02

Get Shortlisted

Our expert panel at KaroStartup evaluates the applications to find the most promising, real businesses.

03

Pitch Live

Present your startup in a closed-room pitch event directly in front of leading investors and funds.

04

Raise & Scale

Secure funding, gain mentorship, and utilize the KaroStartup network to scale your business rapidly.
